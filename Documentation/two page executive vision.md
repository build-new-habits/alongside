## THE PROBLEM: Traditional Fitness Apps Fail Millions

**63% of UK adults are overweight or obese, yet 80% have tried and failed at fitness.**

The problem isn't lack of motivation - it's that fitness apps are designed for a mythical "perfect user" who:
- Has unlimited time and energy
- Never gets injured or experiences pain
- Doesn't have ADHD, anxiety, or depression
- Can follow rigid programs without life getting in the way
- Doesn't menstruate or experience hormonal fluctuations
- Responds well to shame-based motivation ("Don't break your streak!")

**This design philosophy actively excludes:**
- **15-20% of the population** who are neurodivergent (ADHD, autism, anxiety)
- **51% of the population** who menstruate and experience hormonal cycles
- **20 million UK adults** managing chronic injuries and conditions
- **Millions more** juggling careers, families, stress, and mental health challenges

**The result:** People who desperately want to be healthy are failed by tools that shame them for being human.

## THE PSYCHOLOGICAL FOUNDATION

Alongside is grounded in evidence-based psychological frameworks:

### 1. Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan)
**Core Principle:** People are motivated by autonomy, competence, and relatedness - not external pressure.
**Application:** Users choose workouts (3 daily options), exercises matched to skill level, compassionate coaching tone.

### 2. SOLO Taxonomy (Biggs & Collis)
**Core Principle:** Learning progresses through scaffolded levels from simple to complex.
**Application:** Exercise recommendations automatically scale from beginner to advanced as user progresses.

### 3. Mood Meter Science (Marc Brackett, Yale RULER)
**Core Principle:** Emotional awareness requires two dimensions - energy and pleasantness.
**Application:** Check-in captures energy (1-10) and mood (1-10) separately, workouts adapt to both.

### 4. Burnout Research (Dr. Claire Plumbly)
**Core Principle:** Burnout is nervous system dysregulation, detectable through energy patterns.
**Application:** Monitors for 3+ consecutive days with energy â‰¤3, automatically activates "Recovery Mode."
**Reference:** *Burnout: How to Manage Your Nervous System Before It Manages You*, Yellow Kite 2024

### 5. Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
**Core Principle:** Psychological flexibility and values-based action lead to sustainable change.
**Application:** Exercises aligned with user goals, acceptance of current state, commitment within capacity.

## KEY FEATURES AT A GLANCE

### 1. Daily Check-In System (60 seconds)
- Energy slider (1-10): Physical capacity today
- Mood slider (1-10): Emotional state today  
- Sleep tracking (hours + quality)
- Condition updates (pain + difficulty ratings)
- Menstrual cycle day (optional)

### 2. Active Coach: Smart Workout Recommendations
Generates 3 personalized workout options daily:
- **Strength Focus:** Build muscle, power, functional strength
- **Wellbeing Focus:** Mobility, stretching, nervous system regulation
- **Cardio Focus:** Heart health, endurance, calorie burn

Each option includes transparent rationale explaining why it was recommended.

### 3. Hierarchical Condition Management
Not "Do you have injuries? Yes/No" - but "Tell me exactly where, what type, how severe, and how it affects you today."

**3-Level System:**
1. Body region (lower back, knee, shoulder, etc.)
2. Specific location (left side, right side, center)
3. Condition type (disc bulge, muscle strain, arthritis)
4. Daily rating (pain 0-10, difficulty 0-10)

**Exercise Filtering:**
- Pain 8-10: Exercises BLOCKED for that area
- Pain 5-7: Caution warnings, deprioritized
- Pain 1-4: Available with "Manageable" indicator

### 4. Burnout Detection & Recovery Mode
Automatically detects burnout patterns:
- 3+ days of energy â‰¤3
- Rolling 7-day average <4
- Combined low energy + low mood

**Response:** Gentle banner, recovery-only exercises, no pressure to push through.

### 5. Ethical Reward System (No Manipulation)
- Credits for completed exercises (30-120 per exercise)
- NO streaks, NO social comparison, NO FOMO tactics
- Celebrates effort, never punishes humanness
